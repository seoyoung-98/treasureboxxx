When I was young,
I had a birthday party at school, and I wore this tiara and had a birthday party.
The memory of playing with my friends wearing this tiara is so precious that this tiara is precious to me.



There is no reader who can't understand the rather poetic expression of "an unfaded memory stained with emotion."
This is because our lives naturally let us know that very sad or very happy things remain as memories for a long time.
